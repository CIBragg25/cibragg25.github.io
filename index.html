Bio

Anne Bragg was born in Plainfield, NJ to Lester Bragg, Jr. (Father) and Cynthia Bragg (Mother). Anne first discovered her love for the performing arts early in her life, while performing in pageants as a 9 year old girl. She received her undergraduate degree from Morgan State University, and received her graduate degree from New York University.
